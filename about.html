Why we built this

Why we built this

Most students who want to do real independent research, or a serious project of their own, don't know where to start. School rarely teaches that part: how to pick a question, find a mentor and get your bearings in a huge field

How we think about it

How we think about it

  • Your work should be your own — we guide, we don't write it for you.
  • Evidence and testing beat assumptions, every time.
  • Good feedback is how projects improve — seek it out early.
  • Double-check your own claims before anyone else has to.
  • You don't need a title or a credential to ask a real question.
  • Understanding the material matters more than publishing something.
