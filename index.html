The Path to
YOUR North Star.

Polaris is a free, interactive compass designed to help students initiate independent research, ambitious projects, and original inquiries. Instead of doing the work for you, it illuminates how to formulate deep questions, construct rigorous methodologies, and navigate any academic or creative pursuit step-by-step.

Who this is for

Fourteen to eighteen, curious, and tired of waiting for permission.

Maybe you're chasing a science fair placement, a debate resolution, a paper you can't stop thinking about, or an engineering build with no clear first step. Polaris meets you at whichever one it is and gets you moving.

How it works

One navigator, ten kinds of work, a plan shaped around yours.

Tell it what you're building and where you're starting from. It hands back the reading, the schedule, and the next three moves. You do the rest.
